Cake

Description

In this recipe you will learn to make a vanilla cake with white icing and hints of red frosting.

Whether you had a long day or are celebrating with friends this cake is large enough for seven portions.

Ingredients

  • Milk Bucket
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Wheat

Steps

  1. Open up your crafting table
  2. Place three milk buckets in the first row
  3. Place one sugar followed by a space and another sugar in the second row
  4. Place an egg in the the middle
  5. Place three wheats in the third row
