Pumpkin Pie

Description

In this recipe you will learn to make a pumpkin pie that is enough for 4 portions!

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin
  • Sugar
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Open up a crafting table
  2. Place a pumpkin in the first spot in the second row
  3. Place sugar next to it
  4. Place an egg under the sugar
